MSEL Fellows Present Report to Minister Shai Piron

2014-02-24

Fellows from the Mandel School for Educational Leadership brief Israel’s Education Minister on integration of the Ethiopian community 

Fellows from the Mandel School for Educational Leadership presented Education Minister Shai Piron a report on the integration of members of the Ethiopian community in the education system. The group presented and discussed with Minister Peron at the Education Ministry in Tel Aviv on Wednesday, January 29, 2014.

The subject for the report was chosen jointly by the Mandel School for Educational Leadership and the Education Ministry and served as the major three week long group exercise for second year fellows at the School.

The fellows first presented the docum​ent at the Bimat Mandel forum in November 2013​ ​which was attended by MK Amram Mitzna, chair of the Knesset’s Education Committee; MK Shimon Solomon (Yesh Atid party); and Dalit Stauber, former director general of the Ministry of Education, along with key figures from the education system, local government, schools, and academia.
