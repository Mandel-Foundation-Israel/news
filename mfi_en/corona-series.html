A lecture by Professor Manuel Trajtenberg presented as part of the Mandel series "A Time of Crisis: A Time for Leadership"¬†

The COVID-19 crisis is both global and personal at the same time. Its mandatory social distancing and ongoing uncertainty affect all parts of society ‚Äì young and old, in the center and the periphery. The situation demands that those in leadership positions create order within the chaos, find existing anchors to cling to and invent new ones. As Israelis sheltered in place, the Mandel Foundation‚ÄìIsrael launched a series of online enrichment events for the Mandel community. These virtual meetings provided an opportunity to examine dramatic events and big questions from various angles relevant to leaders in educational and social action in Israel.
